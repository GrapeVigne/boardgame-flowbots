Does the FLN have 8 or less remaining resources?

The FLN will try to March its guerrillas as long as the following conditions are met:

1. It has resources remaining. If resources reach 0, the FLN will try to Extort.

2. There is a valid target.

3. At FLN bases there will still be two or more guerrillas remaining after the March.

4. In spaces without bases they will not March out their last underground guerrilla.

5. In spaces with support they will not March out their last guerrilla.

6. In population 1+ spaces, the FLN will only March out guerrillas until they would give the Government player control of the space.
