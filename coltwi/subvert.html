    • Special Activity - Subvert:

    • Subversion removes Algerian cubes, or replaces them with Guerrillas.
    • It may take place in up to two spaces, with at least one Underground Guerrilla and any Algerian cubes.
    • General procedure: either:

      • - remove a total of up to two Algerian cubes (Troops or Police) from among the selected spaces, or;

      - in one space replace one Algerian Police cube with an Underground Guerrilla.

    • Removed or replaced pieces go to Available.
    • Important: the Guerrilla is not Activated.

    Select the appropriate response to progress:

    Is the FLN only allowed a Limited Operation?


    Has the FLN already used a Special Activity (Extort, Subvert or Ambush) this turn?


    The FLN will attempt to Subvert in up to two spaces with both of:

    1. At least one Algerian Cube.

    2. At least one Underground Guerrilla.


    The FLN will select targets in the following order:

    1. To remove the last Cube from up to two spaces (Police before Troops).

    2. In one space replace one Algerian Police with a Guerrilla.

    3. If the Government has already played and at least one piece has been removed per points 1 and 2 above, then in a random space where able.


    Did the FLN Subvert at least once?



    Has the FLN already tried to Extort this turn?

    The FLN will not be able to use Subvert this turn.
