    • Selecting a Random Space

    Two spaces:

    Decide which is your first and which is your second space choice. Now roll a die.

  • On the result of 1, 2 or 3 the first space is selected.
  • On the result of 4, 5 or 6 the second space is selected.

    • Three spaces:

    Decide which is your first, which is your second, and which is your third space choice. Now roll a die.

  • On the result of 1 or 2 the first space is selected.
  • On the result of 3 or 4 the second space is selected.
  • On the result of 5 or 6 the third space is selected.

    • Multiple spaces:

    If you need to select randomly from several spaces on the map, then click the "Select" button below.
