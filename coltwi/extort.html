    • Special Activity - Extort:

    • Extort enables the FLN to gain Resources from areas they inhabit.
    • They may Extort in any spaces with Pop 1 or more that have both at least one Underground Guerrilla and FLN Control.
    • This includes Morocco and Tunisia when these countries become independent.
    • General procedure: activate one Underground Guerrilla to add 1 to FLN Resources.

    The FLN will attempt to Extort at any spaces that meet the following requirements:

    1. The space has 1+ Population.

    2. There are Underground Guerrillas present.


    The FLN will not activate the last Underground Guerrilla at any of its Algerian Bases.


    The spaces will be chosen in the following order, exhausting all possibilities at each level before checking the next:

    1. Firstly, where there are 2+ Guerrillas present. If this is at an FLN Base and there are Cubes there, then only if 3+ Guerrillas.

    2. In Morocco and Tunisia.

    3. If still at zero Resources, then in any and every space possible.
