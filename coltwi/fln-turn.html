    • FLN Bot Turn

    When running the bot:

    • Normal Resource costs, requirements, and procedures still apply.
    • Place friendly pieces from Out of Play first, then from Available. Place Bases before Guerrillas
    • When placing from the map, place only Active Guerrilla pieces from the map to other spaces on the map. Thereby:

      - never remove either of the last two Guerrillas from any FLN Base nor from a Support space.

      - remove first from where there are the most Guerrillas.

    • Remove and replace Guerrillas before Bases, Active Guerrillas before Underground.
    • If there is a choice, remove enemy Bases, then French before Algerians, then Troops before Police.
    • If there is a choice, reduce Commitment first, then Support, then the France Track (toward box “F” only), then Government Resources.
    • When shifting Support/Opposition, first achieve the greatest combined shift in Support and Opposition. Then:

      - removing Terror markers only if also able to achieve a shift.

      - otherwise affect shifts of Support first, then Opposition.
