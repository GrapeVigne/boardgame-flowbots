About the Flowbot Project:

Many board games can be played solo using some kind of artificial intelligence, often nicknamed a 'bot'. These vary greatly in complexity, with some of the more complicated involving flowcharts to simulate the A.I. decision making.

As a big fan of the GMT Games COIN series who would often try to tackle the games so, I became very familiar with the flowcharts and A.I. involved. Personally, while maybe not perfect, I find them to be excellent ways to play the game. However, following the flowcharts was not always the easiest of experiences.

The main pitfalls I personally found were, firstly, that the text on the flowchart itself was often truncated, and so not always clear. Secondly, the flowchart itself could be a little tricky to navigate.

Well, I have recently been learning a little bit of web development, and so decided to take one of the GMT Games (Colonial Twilight as it happened) and see if I could turn the flowchart into a webpage that walked you through each bot turn, step by step. One think led to another, and one game became several, and the monster you have here was born.

My sincere hope is that some other players out there may find this useful and enjoyable to use also, and that some of those solo bots will get to see a little more play time.
